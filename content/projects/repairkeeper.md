## The Problem

Every CRM and job management tool I tried was built for generic service businesses. They all missed the details that matter in a repair shop — tracking parts, managing diagnostics, handling devices that come back, knowing at a glance what's on the bench and what's waiting on a customer.

## The Solution

RepairKeeper is the software I always wanted. Built specifically for electronics repair businesses, and used every day to run DannStarr Electronics.

## What It Does

- Job tracking from intake to collection
- Customer management
- Repair workflow tools
- Built for the realities of a repair shop counter

## Status

Live and in active development. I use it daily — so bugs get fixed fast and features get built because I actually need them.
